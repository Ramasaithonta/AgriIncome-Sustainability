Schematic:

DHT11:

Data (OUT) ? Digital pin 4
VCC ? 5V
GND ? GND
Ultrasonic Sensor (HC-SR04):
Trig ? Digital pin 2
Echo ? Digital pin 3
VCC ? 5V
GND ? GND

Soil Moisture Sensor:

Analog Output ? Analog pin A0
VCC ? 5V
GND ? GND

LCD (I2C):

SDA ? SDA pin on Arduino
SCL ? SCL pin on Arduino
VCC ? 5V
GND ? GND
